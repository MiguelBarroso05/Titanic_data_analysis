Titanic Info

The Titanic project aims to explore and analyze the Titanic dataset, containing information about the passengers and survivors of the infamous disaster. Using Python libraries like Pandas and Matplotlib, the dataset will be manipulated and visualized to provide an in-depth analysis.

The Titanic, a British transatlantic ship, sank on its maiden voyage on April 14-15, 1912, after hitting an iceberg in the North Atlantic Ocean. Carrying around 2,200 passengers and crew, about 1,500 lost their lives, making it one of the deadliest maritime disasters in modern history.

The dataset focuses on a subset of 400 passengers, providing details such as age, gender, class, and survival status. This data allows for a thorough examination of the factors influencing survival rates during the tragedy.
